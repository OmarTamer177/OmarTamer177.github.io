Pro Plast company was established in Egypt 1995. The company has been working with the local market requirements and developing products according to the market needs, putting in mind customer satisfaction as a top priority. Our company also exports to major countries in Europe ,Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Sudan, Syria ,and Lybia. We produce all kinds of straws for commercial or industrial uses. Commercial products includes straws with flexible , artistic, loose, wide, long, or short range with different sizes and colours. Industrial products include straws and sticks for juice factories ,cotton buds factories, ice cream factories, and lollipops factories.

Our mission is to help people find the best suitable products for their disposable needs.

Our goal is to develop new products to meet customer needs and to provide better services for existing ones . Also, plans are made to increase the exports for more countries .
